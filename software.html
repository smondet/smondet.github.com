Pieces Of Software

Among the few acceptable programming languages (ML, Haskell, Ada), OCaml is for me the best compromise between safety, performance, and fun. I have collected some links about the Caml system, and made available some pieces of code:

Sebib, i.e. “S-Expressions for BIBliography”, is a hackable “Bibliographic References Management System”.

Bracetax is a simple and deterministic text-processing syntax for wikis and more.

YaBooN, is Yet Another Bunch Of Ocaml Modules.

locoseq is (or was) a midi-sequencer designed for live performance (development stalled).
